Comprehensive Women's Health at Goodwell Multispeciality Hospital

Expert Gynecology, Obstetrics, and Reproductive Endocrinology Services in East Bengaluru

Welcome to Goodwell Multispeciality Hospital’s Women’s Health Department, where we offer specialized care in gynecology, obstetrics, and reproductive endocrinology (infertility). Our dedicated team of experts is committed to providing comprehensive and compassionate care for women at every stage of life.

Comprehensive Gynecology Services
  • Routine Check-ups and Screenings: Annual exams, Pap smears, HPV testing, and breast exams.
  • Menstrual Disorders: Diagnosis and treatment of irregular periods, heavy bleeding, and painful menstruation.
  • Pelvic Pain: Evaluation and management of chronic pelvic pain and related conditions.
  • Menopause Management: Hormone replacement therapy and other treatments to manage menopause symptoms.
  • Gynecologic Surgeries: Minimally invasive procedures, including laparoscopy and hysterectomy, for various gynecological conditions.
Constant SupervisionExpert Obstetric Care and Care
  • Prenatal Care: Regular check-ups, ultrasounds, and prenatal testing to ensure a healthy pregnancy.
  • High-Risk Pregnancy Management: Specialized care for pregnancies with potential complications.
  • Labor and Delivery: Supportive and personalized care during childbirth, with options for natural delivery, epidural, and cesarean section.
  • Menopause Management: Hormone replacement therapy and other treatments to manage menopause symptoms.
  • Postnatal Care: Comprehensive care for new mothers, including breastfeeding support and postpartum recovery.
