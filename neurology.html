Medical Neurology at Goodwell Multispeciality Hospital

Leading In Neurological Care

Welcome to Goodwell Multispeciality Hospital’s Medical Neurology Department, where we specialize in diagnosing and treating a wide array of neurological disorders. Our team of highly trained neurologists and healthcare professionals is dedicated to providing exceptional care to ensure the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Diagnosis and Treatment of Neurological Disorders
  • Stroke Management: Rapid and effective treatment for acute strokes to minimize long-term impact and improve recovery outcomes.
  • Epilepsy Care: Comprehensive management of seizure disorders, including medication, lifestyle advice, and advanced treatment options.
  • Headache and Migraine: Specialized care for chronic headaches and migraines, focusing on prevention and symptom relief.
  • Parkinson’s Disease: Expert care for movement disorders, including medication management and advanced therapies.
  • Multiple Sclerosis: Comprehensive care for multiple sclerosis patients, including symptom management and rehabilitation.
  • Dementia and Alzheimer’s Disease: Diagnosis, treatment, and support for patients with cognitive decline and memory disorders.
  • Neuropathy and Nerve Disorders: Treatment for peripheral nerve disorders, including diabetic neuropathy and carpal tunnel syndrome.
Multidisciplinary Approach to Neurological Health
  • Neuromuscular Disorders: Comprehensive care for conditions affecting the muscles and the nerves that control them.
  • Neonatal Resuscitation: Immediate and effective resuscitation procedures for newborns in critical condition. Preterm Infant Care: Specialized care for preterm infants to ensure proper growth and development.
  • Movement Disorders: Expert care for Parkinson’s disease, tremors, dystonia, and other movement-related conditions.
  • Sleep Disorders: Diagnosis and treatment of sleep-related neurological disorders, including sleep apnea and insomnia.
  • Autonomic Nervous System Disorders: Treatment for conditions affecting the autonomic nervous system, including fainting and blood pressure regulation issues.
  • Neurogenetics: Specialized care for inherited neurological disorders, including genetic counseling and testing.
