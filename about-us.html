Goodwell Multispeciality Hospital in Kothanur is a trusted healthcare center, offering expert care with compassion and integrity. Located in North Bangalore, we provide personalized treatment plans tailored to your needs.

We are committed to delivering healthcare with integrity and professionalism, focusing on ethical practices and clinical excellence to enhance the health and well-being of every patient we serve.

  • Our 100-bed hospital features advanced facilities designed to provide comprehensive care for all patients.
  • We have a 10-bed ICU dedicated to critical care with round-the-clock expert monitoring and support.
  • A 20-bed rehabilitation unit offers modern physiotherapy and recovery services for post-operative and trauma patients.
