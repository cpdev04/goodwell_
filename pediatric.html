Pediatric and Neonatology

Exceptional Pediatric and Neonatal Services

Welcome to Goodwell Multispeciality Hospital’s Pediatric and Neonatology Department, where we provide exceptional care for infants, children, and adolescents. Our team of highly skilled pediatricians and neonatologists is dedicated to ensuring the best possible outcomes for our young patients through advanced treatments and compassionate care.

Dedicated Pediatric Care
  • Growth and Development Monitoring: Regular check-ups to monitor your child’s growth and developmental milestones.
  • Vaccinations: Comprehensive immunization services to protect your child from various infectious diseases.
  • Preventive Healthcare: Routine screenings and preventive measures to ensure overall health and well-being.
  • 24/7 Emergency Care: Round-the-clock emergency services to address any urgent health concerns.
Expert Care for Newborns
  • Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU): State-of-the-art NICU equipped to handle premature births, low birth weight, and other critical conditions.
  • Neonatal Resuscitation: Immediate and effective resuscitation procedures for newborns in critical condition. Preterm Infant Care: Specialized care for preterm infants to ensure proper growth and development.
  • Congenital Anomalies Management: Diagnosis and treatment of congenital anomalies and birth defects.
